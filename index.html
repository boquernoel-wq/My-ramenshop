Details of the shop

Xian Long Ramen

Xian Long Ramen is a renowned ramen shop that has been serving authentic Japanese ramen for over a decade. Our chefs are dedicated to crafting the perfect bowl of ramen, using traditional techniques and the finest ingredients.

Store Operating Hours

Our store is open from 10:00 AM to 2:00 AM, Monday through Sunday. We welcome you to visit us during these hours to enjoy our delicious ramen and experience the warm ambiance of our shop.
